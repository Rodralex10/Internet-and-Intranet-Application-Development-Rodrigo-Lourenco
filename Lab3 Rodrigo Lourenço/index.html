VLCI Žilina is a well-regarded ice hockey club based in Žilina, Slovakia. Competing in the Slovak Extraliga, the team is known for its strong performances and loyal fanbase. The club plays its home games at the Štadión pod Dubňom, where the atmosphere is always lively and supportive. VLCI Žilina is dedicated to developing young talent and maintaining a competitive team, continuing to be a significant force in Slovak ice hockey.
